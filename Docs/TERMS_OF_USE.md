# DIR DIVING Terms of Use

Last updated: 2026-05-25

## Purpose

DIR DIVING is a companion app for dive logging, preliminary planning review, safety acknowledgment, and watch/phone synchronization.

DIR DIVING is **not** a certified dive computer, does not replace training, and must not be used as a primary life-support instrument.

## Allowed use

You may use the app to:

- review dive logs
- import and export compatible dive data
- review indicative planning outputs
- sync information between Apple Watch and iPhone
- store user-entered notes, equipment, and reference images

## Not allowed / not guaranteed

The app does not guarantee:

- decompression correctness
- underwater GPS availability
- depth sensor availability on every device
- uninterrupted watch/phone synchronization
- suitability as a replacement for certified dive instruments

You remain responsible for:

- using certified and redundant equipment
- following training, local procedures, and dive-center rules
- validating any plan with certified instruments and qualified human judgment

## Underwater and sensor limitations

Some features depend on Apple hardware, Apple entitlements, watchOS APIs, sensors, permissions, and connectivity. Availability can vary by device, environment, and configuration.

## Data and exports

Dive logs, notes, sync metadata, and exports are provided as-is. Exported files are intended for compatibility workflows such as Subsurface CSV, but you are responsible for validating exported data before relying on it.

## No warranty

The app is provided as-is, without any warranty of fitness for dive safety, decompression management, or life-support use.
