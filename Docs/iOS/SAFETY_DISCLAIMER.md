# Safety disclaimer

DIR DIVING iOS Companion includes gas planning and a simplified Bühlmann ZHL-16C no-decompression assistant.

It must not be used as:
- a primary dive computer,
- a certified decompression planner,
- a replacement for agency tables,
- a replacement for technical-diving training,
- a replacement for team procedures.

The calculations are preliminary, educational, and informational only.
Always follow certified training, redundant instruments, validated planning tools, and conservative team procedures.

## In-app safety framing

The stable iOS Planner and Plan Result screens must keep visible in-app warning text explaining that the planner is informational and not certified. The warning belongs in the user flow, not only in documentation, because the UI displays technical-looking outputs such as deco stops, CNS/OTU and Bühlmann-style curves.

Do not remove this in-app safety framing unless the planner is replaced by a validated and certified planning workflow.
