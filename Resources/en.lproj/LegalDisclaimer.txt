IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTICE

DIR Diving is NOT a dive computer. DIR Diving is a dive companion and monitoring application designed exclusively to display and record selected dive-related information such as depth, average depth, runtime, water temperature, GPS surface position, telemetry, navigation support, and dive logs. The application must never be used as the sole instrument for dive planning, decompression management, life-support decisions, emergency management, ascent decisions, gas management, or technical diving procedures.

SENSOR AND DEVICE LIMITATIONS

DIR Diving relies on Apple Watch sensors, watchOS APIs, Bluetooth communication, GPS systems, motion sensors, and depth/pressure sensors. These systems may malfunction, freeze, disconnect, or provide inaccurate readings without warning. Environmental conditions may affect the operation and reliability of the application.

TRAINING REQUIREMENT

DIR Diving is intended only for certified divers with appropriate training for the dives they perform. The app does not replace professional instruction, certification, or safe diving procedures.

APPLE WATCH NOTICE

DIR Diving is an independent application and is not certified, endorsed, or approved by Apple as a certified diving computer. Compatibility with Apple Watch models does not imply certification for life-support or decompression use.

MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

Diving exposes users to physiological stress and medical risks. Consult a qualified physician before diving. Never dive under the influence of alcohol, drugs, fatigue, or incompatible medical conditions.

DATA AND LOGGING DISCLAIMER

Dive logs, GPS tracks, telemetry, exported files, and synchronization data may contain inaccuracies or incomplete information. Such data must never be used as the sole basis for decompression analysis or medical evaluation.
